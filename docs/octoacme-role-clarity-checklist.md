# OctoAcme — Role & Responsibility Clarity Checklist

## Purpose
Ensure that at project initiation, all roles are clearly defined, responsibilities are non-overlapping, and team members know who does what. This checklist prevents delays, rework, and finger-pointing caused by unclear accountability.

## When to Use
Complete this checklist during **Project Initiation** or at the start of any project with multiple roles.

## Phase 8: Success Metrics & Accountability

### How Will We Know Roles Are Working Well?

- [ ] Clear ownership of all decisions? (Check: No confusion about who decides)
- [ ] Minimal escalations / fast resolution? (Target: ___% of issues resolved at Level 1)
- [ ] Smooth handoffs between phases? (Check: No blockers at handoff points)
- [ ] Team confidence in role clarity? (Survey after kickoff)
- [ ] Low rework due to misunderstanding? (Track rework %; Target: ____%)
- [ ] On-time delivery? (Track vs. plan; Target: ____%)

### Retrospective Questions

After first sprint, ask team:
- Was role clarity helpful in reducing delays?
- Were there any unclear responsibilities that caused rework?
- Do we need to adjust who owns what?
- Are meetings at the right frequency and with the right people?
- Are escalations being handled appropriately?
